Exploring the Moon and Beyond

Introduction

The Apollo program was a series of space missions conducted by NASA between 1961 and 1972. The primary goal was to land humans on the Moon and bring them back safely to Earth, which was successfully achieved by Apollo 11 in 1969. These missions are considered some of the most iconic and groundbreaking achievements in human history.
